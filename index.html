Questions

FAQ

A few common questions before you hit submit. Still unsure? Ask anything in the form and we’ll cover it during your consult.

Do you take walk‑ins?

Art Monster is primarily appointment‑based so artists have time to prep and design. Walk‑ins are sometimes available for small pieces — call or DM the shop the day of to check.

How far out are you booking?

Smaller tattoos can often be booked within a couple of weeks. Larger projects like sleeves or back pieces may be scheduled several weeks or months out, depending on the artist.

What should I bring to my consultation?

Bring reference images, rough sketches, or even notes on what the tattoo represents to you. Knowing your preferred size, placement, and budget also helps us move quickly.

Do you require a deposit?

Yes. A non‑refundable deposit is required to secure your appointment and is applied to the final price of your tattoo. If you need to reschedule, we simply require adequate notice.

Is your studio safe and licensed?

Absolutely. We follow strict sanitation protocols, use single‑use needles and tubes, and operate in compliance with local health department regulations for tattoo studios.

Can you help with aftercare?

Every client leaves with written aftercare instructions tailored to the size and placement of their tattoo, plus direct contact info if anything feels off while you’re healing.

Ready to start your next piece?

Tell us what you’re envisioning and we’ll match you with the best artist for your style, budget, and timeline. No pressure, no hard sell — just an honest game plan.
